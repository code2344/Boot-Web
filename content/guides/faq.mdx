### What are the requirements?

#### From Scratch OS

- It must boot
- It must be original
- It must be able to print "Hello World"
- It must be able to run 2 different commands

#### Based Off OS

- It must boot
- It must be original
- It must be able to edit/create a .txt file.

<Callout title="Info">
  These requirements are a bare minimum. Please go far and beyond these
  requirements.
</Callout>

### How is scoring determined?

Your OS will be graded in 3 categories, 0-5 stars.

#### Categories

##### From Scratch OS

- Technicality (How technical is the OS)
- Functionality (How functional is the OS)
- UX (How easy and intuitive is the OS)

##### Based Off OS

- Technicality (How technical are the modified parts of the OS)
- Originality (How different is this OS, and does it fix a meaningful issue or meaningfully differentiate itself from the base OS)
- UX (How easy and intuitive are the modified parts of the OS)

### How do you earn Boot Coins?

For every hour you work on your OS, you get 30 Boot Coins. You can get a multiplier based on your OS Level (From Scratch, Based On) and how your OS gets graded. You can also get more rewards from completing bounties.

### Can I use AI?

You can use AI to help you, as long as you declare what you use it on your Project's AI statement section. However you can NOT vibe code the entire thing. We want to see your creativity, not just AI slop.
